# Prioritization Template

## Candidate item
What are we deciding on?

## Problem
What user or business problem does it address?

## Impact
What benefit will it create?

## Reach
How many users or teams are affected?

## Confidence
How confident are we in the expected impact?

## Effort
How hard is it to deliver?

## Risks
What could go wrong?

## Dependencies
What must happen first?

## Priority decision
- Now
- Next
- Later
- Drop

## Rationale
Why did we choose this priority?
