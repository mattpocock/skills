# Problem Framing Template

## Problem
What is the problem in plain language?

## Who has the problem
Which user segment is affected?

## Evidence
What data, feedback, or incidents show this is a real problem?

## Why now
Why is this important at this moment?

## Desired outcome
What should be better if we solve it?

## Constraints
What constraints must we respect?

## Options
What are the possible ways to address this?

## Recommendation
What approach should we take first?
