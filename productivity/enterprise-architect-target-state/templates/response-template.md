# Enterprise Architecture Response Template

Use this template to answer architecture requests in a consistent, executive-ready way.

## 1. Executive conclusion
- State the recommendation in 1-3 sentences.
- Be direct about proceed / pause / redesign / standardize / phase.

## 2. Current state
- What exists today?
- What constraints or bottlenecks matter?
- What risks or duplication are present?

## 3. Target state
- What should the enterprise look like at scale?
- What are the key architectural principles?
- What patterns should be standardized?

## 4. Gap analysis
- What is misaligned between current and target state?
- What technical gaps exist?
- What organizational or governance gaps exist?

## 5. Transition roadmap
- Wave 1: stabilize / contain risk
- Wave 2: standardize / migrate
- Wave 3: optimize / retire legacy
- List dependencies and critical path items

## 6. Risks and tradeoffs
- What improves?
- What is sacrificed?
- What assumptions drive the recommendation?

## 7. Governance
- What standards or policies apply?
- What approvals are required?
- Are any exceptions needed?

## 8. Next actions
- List immediate steps.
- Keep them concrete and assigned to the right teams.
